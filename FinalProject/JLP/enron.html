This matrix diagram visualizes Enron Employee interoffice email exchanges derived from the Enron Email Corpus. The largest readily available corporate email database with over 600,000 emails generated by 158 employees. Enron Email Corpus.

Each colored cell represents two employees that exchanged emails. Cells are color coded by overall sender volume.

Orange > 2,001

Green between 1,001 - 2,000

Red between 501 - 1000

Black between 251 - 500

Purple between 101 - 250

Pink between 51 - 100

Grey sent Zero within this group but received over 50

Each person listed has a cell filled with their sent color when their names intersect.

Use the drop-down menu to reorder the matrix and explore the data.
